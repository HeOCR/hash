Here is the complete and detailed list of the public domain and open-access resources containing post-1929 handwritten Hebrew scans, formatted as requested:

# Institutional Archives & Primary Source Scans

## National Library of Israel (NLI)

The NLI holds extensive public domain manuscripts, definitively clearing the Life + 70 years copyright threshold for authors who died before 1956.

**Shaul Tchernichovsky (1875–1943)**

* **Seven handwritten receipts:** Everyday cursive handwriting from the author's later life.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912210205171/NLI](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912210205171/NLI) 




* **"The Bandage" (התחבושת):** Handwritten literary draft.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912380205171/NLI](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912380205171/NLI) 




* **Hybrid Notebook (מחברת-שעטנז):** A mixture of notes and observations.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035403420205171/NLI](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035403420205171/NLI) 




* **Draft of a Memorandum (טיוטת זכרון דברים):** Functional, administrative handwritten notes.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912230205171/NLI](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.nli.org.il/he/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990035912230205171/NLI) 





**Henrietta Szold (1860–1945)**

* **Letter to the Eretz Israel Conservatory of Music (August 31, 1943):** Administrative Hebrew correspondence.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007268882105171](https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007268882105171) 




* **Radio Speech to the Children of Eretz Israel (1940):** Handwritten speech notes delivered on Jerusalem Radio.
* URL: [https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007268882105171](https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007268882105171) 





## Jabotinsky Institute Archive

**Ze'ev Jabotinsky (1880–1940)**

* **"The Hebrew Accent - Handwritten and Brochure" (1930):** Notes concerning linguistic phonetics (Reference Code: A 1 - 7/ 82).
* URL: [https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/search-archive/item/?itemId=115024](https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/search-archive/item/?itemId=115024) 




* **Population Exchange Handwritten Notes (1939):** Late-life political drafts (Reference Code: A 1 - 12/ 2).
* URL: [https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/catalog-of-files/?section=A&arc=9704&page=78](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/catalog-of-files/%3Fsection%3DA%26arc%3D9704%26page%3D78) 





## Kenyon College Digital Archives

**Henrietta Szold (1860–1945)**

* **Hand-signed Typewritten Letter:** A Hebrew letter featuring an authentic handwritten signature on the stationery of the Jewish Community of Palestine.
* URL: [https://digital.kenyon.edu/bulmash/311/](https://digital.kenyon.edu/bulmash/311/) 





## University of Michigan Digital Collections

**The Rosenberg Family**

* **World War II Era Correspondence (1938-1946):** A collection of 105 personal letters bridging the pre-war and post-war years. The collection's creator dedicated these files entirely to the public domain via the CC0 1.0 designation.
* URL: [https://quod.lib.umich.edu/](https://quod.lib.umich.edu/) 





# Computational Machine Learning Datasets

## Tel Aviv University (TAU) / Figshare

* **Modern Hebrew Baseline Dataset:** A dataset consisting of the handwriting of 18 contemporary individuals, compiled by TAU researchers for algorithmic verification of ancient ostraca. It is released under a highly permissive CC BY 4.0 license.
* URL: [https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.11423352.v1](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.11423352.v1) 





## The Hebrew Handwritten Dataset (HHD)

*(Note: These contain modern, post-1929 handwritten forms but are explicitly restricted to non-commercial/academic use only)*

* **HHD_age (Kaggle):** ~850 handwritten forms for predicting writer demographics, licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.
* URL: [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/liorabergel/hhd-age](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/liorabergel/hhd-age) 




* **HHD_gender (Zenodo):** 819 handwritten forms for algorithmic gender classification.
* URL: [https://zenodo.org/records/4729908](https://zenodo.org/records/4729908) 




* **HHD_v0 (HuggingFace / TC11):** Isolated Hebrew characters from hand-filled forms for neural network training.
* URL: [https://huggingface.co/datasets/sivan22/hebrew-handwritten-dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/sivan22/hebrew-handwritten-dataset) 


* URL: [https://tc11.cvc.uab.es/datasets/HHD_v0_1](https://tc11.cvc.uab.es/datasets/HHD_v0_1) 





# Decentralized Repositories & Aggregators

## Wikimedia Commons

* **Category: Handwritten Hebrew letters:** A community-moderated repository containing original photographic scans of public domain handwriting and vector-based reproductions (SVG).
* URL: [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Handwritten_Hebrew_letters](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Handwritten_Hebrew_letters) 




* **Subcategory: SVG Set:**
* URL: [https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Handwritten_Hebrew_letters_(SVG_set)](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Handwritten_Hebrew_letters_(SVG_set)) 





## Harvard Library Judaica Digital Collection

* **HOLLIS for Archival Discovery:** Harvard's massive digital index containing over 6.3 million images, including public domain personal papers from 1930-1955. Their metadata is released for public use under a CC0 1.0 Public Domain Designation.
* URL (Search Interface): [https://library.harvard.edu/services-tools/hollis-archival-discovery](https://library.harvard.edu/services-tools/hollis-archival-discovery) 


* URL (Collection Details): [https://library.harvard.edu/collections/judaica-digital-collection](https://library.harvard.edu/collections/judaica-digital-collection)
