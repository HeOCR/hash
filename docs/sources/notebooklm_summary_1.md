### 1. Institutional Repositories & State Archives

* **The National Library of Israel (NLI) / Ktiv Portal**
  * **Ktiv Database**: Aggregates over 400,000 digitized Hebrew manuscripts globally, encompassing a wide array of personal and historical archives.
    * URL: https://www.nli.org.il/en/discover/manuscripts/hebrew-manuscripts
  * **Hannah Senesh Archive**: Features public domain scans of her 1936 handwritten poem "The Tear", her personal diary, and agricultural notes taken at the Girls' Agricultural Training Farm in Nahalal in the 1940s.
    * URL: https://www.nli.org.il/en/archives/nnl_archive_al997010399887105171/NLI
  * **Stefan Zweig Collection**: Contains 21 handwritten and typed letters as well as 12 postcards corresponding with Martin Buber from the 1930s.
    * URL: https://www.nli.org.il/en/archives/NNL_ARCHIVE_AL990033646240205171/NLI
  * **Max Meyerhof & Leon Reich Archives**: Digitized personal papers, manuscript drafts, and letters dating from the early 20th century.
    * Max Meyerhof URL: https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007277698705171
    * Leon Reich URL: https://www.nli.org.il/en/a-topic/987007267064705171

* **The Israel State Archives (ISA)**
  * Serves as the central repository for the State of Israel and the British Mandate; recently digitized 380,000 pages related to the Eichmann Trial, which include handwritten state correspondence from Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion now in the public domain.
  * EHRI Portal ISA URL: https://portal.ehri-project.eu/institutions/il-004883
  * Eichmann Trial Collection News URL: https://www.timesofisrael.com/israel-national-archive-opens-online-access-to-380000-pages-of-eichmann-trial-documents/

* **Israel Antiquities Authority (IAA) Archive**
  * Contains digitized scientific records, field notes, and administrative files with signatures and handwritten marginalia from the British Mandate period (1919–1948).
  * URL: https://archives.iaa.org.il/

* **The IDF Archives**
  * Holds approximately 17 million files, including unclassified, public-domain handwritten military reports, logs, and soldier diaries from the 1948 War of Independence.
  * Overview/Background URL: https://imes.elliott.gwu.edu/public-outreach-and-educator-resources/impop/israels-vanishing-files-archival-deception-and-paper-trails/

### 2. Archives of Prominent Zionists & Literary Figures

* **Hayim Nahman Bialik (1873–1934)**
  * Highly accessible handwritten letters, such as a 1934 document outlining the need for a home in Zion, and a 1924 letter requesting medical help for a sick child.
  * Shapell Manuscript Foundation URL: https://www.shapell.org/collection/jewish-history/bialik-hayim-nahman/
  * Kedem Auctions URL: https://www.kedem-auctions.com/en/hayim-nahman-bialik-letter-jacob-fichman-signed-postcard
  * Farkash Gallery URL: https://farkash-gallery.com/our-shop/rare-documents/known-figures-documents/hayim-nahman-bialik-rare-letter-1924/

* **Ze'ev Jabotinsky (1880–1940)**
  * Extensively preserved notes and letters, including 1928 handwritten speech notes for a Betar officers' conference, a 1938 letter begging for funds to save European Jews, and various personal records.
  * Betar Speech Notes URL: https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/search-archive/item/?itemId=115421
  * General Catalog URL: https://en.jabotinsky.org/archive/catalog-of-files/?section=A&arc=9704&page=2
  * 1938 Letter News URL: https://www.timesofisrael.com/jabotinsky-letter-revealing-plea-for-funds-to-save-european-jews-heads-to-auction/

* **Chaim Weizmann (1874–1952)**
  * A vast collection of letters and manuscripts detailing diplomatic and personal matters throughout the early to mid-20th century, available online and in 23 published volumes.
  * Shapell Manuscript Foundation URL: https://www.shapell.org/collection/jewish-figures/weizmann-chaim/
  * Center for Israel Education (CIE) Archive URL: https://israeled.org/topic/weizmann-letters/
  * Internet Archive (Series B) URL: https://archive.org/details/letterspapersofc0000weiz_s6r6

### 3. Computational Datasets for Handwritten Text Recognition (HTR)

* **HebHTR Dataset**
  * A comprehensive machine learning dataset containing roughly 100,000 words, including 50,000 authentic examples of contemporary Hebrew cursive handwriting extracted from scanned student exams.
  * GitHub URL: https://github.com/Lotemn102/HebHTR

* **Hebrew Handwriting Dataset (HHD)**
  * Focused on modern character and word recognition, this includes the `HHD_age` subset with 850 scanned handwritten forms completed by volunteers of varied ages and backgrounds.
  * HHD_v0_1 TC-11 URL: https://tc11.cvc.uab.es/datasets/HHD_v0_1
  * HHD_age Kaggle URL: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/liorabergel/hhd-age
